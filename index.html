About Me

I am a trilingual professional with experience in coding, research, translation, analysis and financial operations.

I graduated from Brigham Young University where I majored in Political Science and minored in Chinese. I speak fluent Spanish and professional-level Mandarin Chinese.

My career since graduating has been in the financial services industry, and I am now transitioning to coding. I am currently attending The Coding Boot-camp Full Stack Flex Program at the University of Utah.
